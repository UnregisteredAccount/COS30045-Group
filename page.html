Project Title: Cardiovascular Disease

4 charts related to our project

Presented by Lim Chee Yung & Gian Tze Ee

Welcome to our data visualization project, which delves into the critical and complex topic of cardiovascualr disease. As one of the leading causes of death worldwide, cardiovascular disease affects millions and imposes significant social, economic, and healthcare burdens.

Using four data visualizations, we aim to shed light on key aspects of cardiovascular disease, inlcuding its prevalence, risk factors, trends over time, and impacts on different populations. These insigthts are essential for understanding the scope of the issue and for informing efforts to reduce the incidence of cardiovascular disease and improve public health outcomes.
